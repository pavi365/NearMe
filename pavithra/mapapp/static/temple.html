AVADI

Nemam Amma Bhagavan Temple


Nemam Amma Bhagavan Temple is a "powerful energy station" created by Shri Amma bhagawan. Some say it is a sacred place that offers solutions for mankind, while others say it is a place for experiencing miracles. The temple is located at the confluence of several ley lines, and is made of white marble with stupas, spires, domes, and latticed windows. The temple complex has a span of 50 meters and is designed to the Golden Ratio. It has three floors, each of which is a large meditation hall that covers over 22,500 square feet.
