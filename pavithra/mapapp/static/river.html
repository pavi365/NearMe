Thaneerkulam is a nice place to visit and also culture oriented people. full of Green lands are there, Silent place and ground water very sweet Lots of temple festival will be held in year. Few of the temples i am going to mention here :Shiva Temple and also Lord Ganesh temple is located here. Near by village peoples will visit Thaneerkulam for shopping purposes and also people are very nice and kind hearted. Helping attitude you can find in any corner of the Thaneerkulam village thats special. Government school are located in Thaneerkulam and Kurunchi CBSCE School near by village children will study in school.
